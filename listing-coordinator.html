Listing Coordinator Service

Simplifying the listing process from start to finish

Back to Services
$250

Our comprehensive Listing Coordination Service handles every detail of your property listing

Document Collection & Review

  • Gather all necessary listing documents from the agent.
  • Review documents for completeness, including:
    • Listing Contract
    • Seller Contact Details & Marital Status (required by the title company)
    • Showing Instructions
    • Seller's Disclosure Statement (SDS), Lead-Based Paint (LBP), Agency Disclosures
    • Trust Documents, HOA Information, Mortgage Release Authorizations
    • Divorce Decrees or Death Certificates (if applicable)
  • Manage communication with both parties as needed.

Photography Coordination

  • Contact the seller to schedule property photos (if required).
  • Introduce ourselves to the client as the first touchpoint.

MLS Listing Input & Photo Management

  • Input listing details into the MLS with all required fields completed.
  • Sort, name, and upload property photos to MLS.
  • Upload required disclosures for transparency and compliance.

ShowingTime Setup & Listing Review

  • Set up ShowingTime for easy scheduling of property showings.
  • Coordinate with the agent and seller to review the MLS listing for accuracy.
  • Once approved, make the listing live, marking our second touchpoint with the client.

Client Communication & Online Accuracy Check

  • Verify the listing's accuracy on online platforms like Zillow and Realtor.com.
  • Send a congratulatory email to the client with a link to their Zillow listing (third touchpoint).

Pre-Title Order

  • Place the pre-title order once the listing is live, ensuring all steps are covered.

Added Services Available

In addition to our comprehensive listing coordination services, we offer the following add-ons:

  • Lockbox Installation (within 20 miles of Canton, MI)
  • Window Sign, Brochures, Open House Snack Bar, Shoe Sign & Boot Covers - Prices available upon request.
