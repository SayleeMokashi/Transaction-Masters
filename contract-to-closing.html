Contract to Closing Services (For Seller) - $350

What You Can Expect:

Document Review & Preparation:

  • We begin by reviewing all necessary documents in the following order:
    • Seller's Input Sheet: Collect seller contact details, marital status, and current address.
    • Listing Agreement & Addendums (if any).
    • Accepted Purchase Agreement.
    • Disclosures: SDS & LBP completed by seller.
    • Broker to Broker Compensation Document.
    • Agency Disclosure.
    • C of O Documentation (if applicable).

Client Introduction & Communication:

  • After gathering all necessary documents, we introduce ourselves to the seller by:
    • Sending an introductory email (first touchpoint).
    • Calling the seller to personally introduce ourselves (second touchpoint).
    • Simultaneously sending introductory emails to the buyer's agent and title company to ensure alignment.

Sales File and Document Upload:

  • We ensure all documents are properly uploaded to the appropriate systems:
    • Send the sales file to the title company.
    • Upload required documents to broker-specific portals (e.g., Skyslope) to maintain organization.

Scheduling & Inspection Management:

  • Send calendar invites to both the agent and the seller with inspection dates and deadlines.
  • Notify seller of inspection details (third touchpoint) so they can prepare the property.
  • Coordinate access for inspectors and appraisers as needed.

Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) & Inspection Response:

  • Confirm receipt of Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) and share the signed receipt with seller.
  • After the inspection, we:
    • Review the buyer's inspection response.
    • Prepare necessary repair negotiation documents.
    • Submit final agreements to the title company and lender.

Appraisal & Transaction Monitoring:

  • Notify seller of appraisal appointment (fourth touchpoint).
  • Coordinate with the seller to ensure property access for the appraiser.
  • Review title work and communicate with the seller if title issues need to be addressed.

Ongoing Communication & Follow-ups:

  • We continue to manage the transaction by:
    • Following up on appraisal results and preparing any necessary price change addendums.
    • Monitoring loan progress with buyer's lender.
    • Managing any repair documentation required from contractors.
    • Touch base with the seller for updates on the closing timeline (fifth touchpoint).

Finalizing the Closing:

  • Once mortgage approval is confirmed, we:
    • Schedule the closing.
    • Send the closing details to both the agent and the seller, adding it to their calendars.
    • Confirm final payoff figures are accurate and communicated to the title company.
    • Send closing documents and the ALTA for review to all parties.

Closing Preparation:

  • Schedule the walk-through with the buyer's agent.
  • Confirm with the title agency that seller closing documents are ready.
  • Send utilities transfer reminder to the seller (sixth touchpoint) to ensure a smooth transition.

Key Exchange & Final Communication:

  • If there is post-closing occupancy, we coordinate on key exchange timeline.
  • If there is no occupancy, we ensure the seller leaves all house keys, garage door openers, and access devices for the closing.
  • After closing, we send a congratulations email to the seller and include a link for a Google review for both the client and Transaction Masters.
