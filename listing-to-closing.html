Listing to Closing Services for Sellers

Our Listing to Closing service for sellers ensures every detail is taken care of from the moment you list your property until the deal is sealed. For just $500, we handle all the coordination, paperwork, and follow-up, so you can focus on what matters most.

What We Offer:

Listing Coordination & Document Review:

  • We start by procuring all necessary listing documents from the agent and reviewing them for completeness. These documents include:
    • Listing Contract
    • Seller contact details & marital status (required by the title company)
    • Showing instructions (call, text, email, etc.)
    • Disclosures: SDS, LBP, Agency
    • Broker compliance documents (wire fraud, etc.)
    • Trust documents (updated certificate of trust, ensuring a valid bank account)
    • Condo/HOA Docs (if applicable)
    • Mortgage Release Authorization
    • Divorce decree or legal letter regarding the split of funds
    • Death certificate (if applicable)
    • Letter of authority (if probate)
    • C of O procedure
  • We ensure that all originals are submitted to the title company at closing and will be returned after a few weeks.

Photography & Marketing Coordination:

  • Once all documents are in order, we begin working with the seller to ensure the property is ready to be marketed. This includes:
    • Scheduling and coordinating photos with the seller (first touchpoint with the client)
    • Ordering yard sign and installing it
    • Inputting listing into MLS
    • Sorting, naming, and uploading photos to MLS
    • Uploading disclosures to MLS to maintain transparency
    • Setting up ShowingTime for scheduling showings
    • Getting MLS reviewed by the agent and sellers
    • Making the listing live once approved by the agent and seller (second touchpoint with the client)

Online Listing Accuracy & Communication:

  • Once the listing goes live, we verify its accuracy on major online platforms such as Zillow and Realtor.com to ensure it's being properly presented.
  • Sending a congratulatory email to the seller with a link to their listing on Zillow (third touchpoint with the client)

Pre-Title Order & Closing Preparation:

  • After the listing goes live, we begin the pre-title process to prepare for a smooth closing:
    • Order pre-title to ensure all legal aspects are in order
    • Communicate regularly with the seller to keep them updated and provide any necessary documents for closing

Additional Add-Ons:

  • We also offer several add-ons to ensure a seamless and efficient process:
    • Lockbox installation (within a 20-mile radius of Canton, MI)
    • Window sign installation
    • Brochures: We will create and deliver property brochures to showcase the home
    • Open House brochures: Create and deliver open house materials
    • Open House snack bar: As per the agent's request
    • Shoe sign & boot covers: To maintain cleanliness during showings

All of these comprehensive services are available for just $500! Contact us today to ensure your property selling process is smooth and stress-free.
