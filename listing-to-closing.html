Contract to Closing Services (For Seller) - $350

Document Review & Preparation:

  • We start by receiving and reviewing all necessary documents in the following order:
    • Seller input sheet: Collect seller contact details, marital status, and current address
    • Purchase agreement & addendums (if any)
    • Lender's pre-approval/proof of funds
    • Disclosures: SDS & LBP
    • Broker to broker compensation document
    • Agency disclosure
    • C of O (if applicable)

Client Communication & Introduction:

  • Once all documents are in order, we begin our communication with the seller and key parties:
    • Introduction email to the buyer (first touchpoint)
    • Call the seller to introduce (second touchpoint)
    • Introduction email to the listing agent
    • Introduction email to the co-op title company
    • Send the sales file to the title company
    • Upload required documents to broker-specific portals (e.g., Skyslope)

Scheduling & Inspection Coordination:

  • We coordinate inspection details and ensure that everything is set for timely action:
    • Send calendar invite to the agent and seller with the inspection date and deadline
    • Send inspection info to the seller (third touchpoint)

Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) & Inspection Follow-up:

  • We ensure that all financial and inspection details are in place:
    • Check on the EMD and obtain the signed receipt, sending a copy to the listing agent
    • Check on the inspection results, and make any necessary addendums per the agent's instructions
    • Send addendum to lender & title company (if required)
    • Get inspection release addendum and send it out

Appraisal & Mortgage Updates:

  • We handle appraisal details and mortgage follow-ups to ensure everything is on track for closing:
    • Send appraisal information to the seller (fourth touchpoint)
    • Check with the lender on appraisal status—if ordered and scheduled
    • Review title work and communicate with the seller if additional info is needed
    • Follow up on appraisal results and make price change addendum if needed
    • Follow up on mortgage approval
    • Follow up on repairs and receipts if required

Ongoing Communication & Closing Preparation:

  • We continue to keep the seller updated and manage all closing logistics:
    • Touch base with the seller to update on the appraisal and final closing plan (fifth touchpoint)
    • Schedule the closing once mortgage approval is obtained
    • Send closing details to the agent and seller, adding the closing to their calendars
    • Follow up with the lender for final figures to send to the buyer
    • Send closing docs & ALTA for review to the agent and buyer

Finalizing the Walk-Through & Closing:

  • We ensure everything is in order for the closing day:
    • Schedule the walk-through on behalf of the agent
    • Confirm with the seller and title agency that the seller funds are in place
    • Send utilities transfer info to the seller (seventh touchpoint)

Key Exchange & Final Communication:

  • We handle the final steps of the transaction:
    • Communicate with the listing agent on key exchange (if there is occupancy)
    • If no occupancy, we'll coordinate with the buyer's agent to bring the lockbox to closing
    • Send congratulations email with a link for a Google review for the client and Transaction Masters
